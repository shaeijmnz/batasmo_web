Atty. Jeanne Luz Castillo-Anarna
-Family Law 
-Corporate and Business Law
-Intellectual Property Law
-General Litigation
Atty. Jeanne obtained her law degree from the Faculty of Civil Law, University of Santo Tomas, where she was awarded with a Medal for Leadership. She holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Legal Management from the same university.
Atty. Jeanne started her law career in private practice where she worked in an Ortigas law firm for more than three years. During her years as a young practitioner, she harnessed her skills in litigation where she handled civil, criminal, and corporate cases and appeared in different courts and tribunals. Atty. Jeanne earned a vast experience in corporate law  where she assisted corporate clients with their contracts, acted as corporate secretary and handled the client’s corporate records such as minutes, resolutions and certificates.
Atty. Jeanne joined the judiciary and was appointed as Branch Clerk of Court in the Regional Trial Court of Quezon City. She clerked in a Family Court for more than three years and gained mastery of the pertinent family laws.
As a Partner in Anarna Law, Atty. Jeanne handles the firm’s litigation department as well as it’s corporate division.

Atty. Alston Kevin Anarna
-Real Estate and Land Registration Law
-Election and Administrative Law
-Labor Law
Atty. Kevin obtained his Bachelor of Laws degree in the University of Santo Tomas. He holds a double degree in Political Science and Legal Management from the De La Salle University Manila. 
After passing the bar examinations, Atty. Kevin immediately immersed himself in the private practice of law. He was active in litigation and represented clients in different courts and tribunals. He was also the legal consultant of the Civil Service Commission and the Department of Health.
In addition to his law practice, Atty. Kevin is active in local governance. He started his career in public service when he was elected as the Provincial Board Member of the 5th District of the Province of Cavite in 2019. As an incumbent Provincial Board Member, he is the Chairman of the Committee on Trade and Industry.

Atty. Allen Kristopher Anarna
-Real Estate and Land Registration Law
-Law on Public Officials
-Administrative Law
-Taxation 
Atty. Allen earned his Bachelor of Law degree from the Faculty of Civil Law of the University of Santo Tomas. He also obtained his Bachelor of Science degree in Accountancy in the same university, and subsequently passed the Board of Examination for Certified Public Accountants. 
Atty. Allen began his legal career as an Attorney for the Presidential Anti-Corruption Commission. While in the PACC, Atty. Allen is actively involved in providing legal support in  the development management, general government administration and internal administration of the different government agencies.
In 2019, Atty. Allen is appointed as the Deputy Register of Deeds of the Registry of Deeds – Taguig City where he continues to serve to this day.
